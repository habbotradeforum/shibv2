Shiboshis Collection

SHIBOSHIS are 10,000 Shiba Inu-generated Non Fungible Tokens (NFTS) that are recorded on the Ethereum blockchain in perpetuity.

SHIBs have distinguishing features that make them collectible (for instance Laser Eyes or Party Hat).

Furthermore, each trait corresponds to a boost in Shiboshi's base ShibaStrength in our upcoming Shiboshi game, "SHIB ETERNITY." These are the Shiba Inu decentralised community's official SHIBOSHIS, as seen on Forbes, Wall Street Journal, Fortune, CNN, MSNBC, and a variety of other media outlets.

Shib The Metaverse

SHIB: The Metaverse is a virtual representation of our collective history, showcasing our ingenuity and camaraderie in the form of a place we can all call home.

We are developing immersive experiences that will allow users to explore, learn from, and interact with the Shiba Inu Universe in unprecedented ways.

Our goal is to provide users with a new and exciting way to earn passive income, collect in-game resources, generate rewards, and even have their own space to build and manage their projects.

Shiba Inu Games (Shiba Eternity)

Shiba Eternity is a free-to-play collectable card game lovingly crafted by PlaySide Studios under the guidance of William Volk, and under the creative direction of Shytoshi Kusama. Shiba Eternity plunges you headlong into the exciting world of Shiboshis; a special race of Shiba Inu dogs that have developed technology, art, and warfare of their own.

With over 500 unique cards to collect and 10,000 different Shiboshi heroes, Shiba Eternity allows you to craft your very own deck based on the traits of your Shiboshis and the fighters of six versatile disciplines!
